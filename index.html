About Hydra CF

Hydra CF is more than just a team — we are a movement. Born from passion and built with grind, we represent a new generation of competitive FC Mobile players from India. From local clashes to global leaderboards, our mission is simple: to dominate every tournament we enter and inspire a wave of fearless mobile esports players.

With every match, every goal, and every win, we’re building India’s strongest FC Mobile dynasty — one boot at a time.
